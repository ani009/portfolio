Snake & Ladder Game

The game is played on a square board divided into a grid of squares, typically numbered from 1 to 100, with snakes and ladders placed at certain positions. The objective of the game is simple: Players take turns rolling a dice and moving their tokens along the numbered squares according to the number rolled. The first player to reach the final square (usually 100) wins the game.
